About Us

Everguide Logistics is a premier airfreight and cargo solutions provider headquartered at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA), Nairobi. With over a decade of expertise, we specialize in fast, reliable airfreight services serving regional and international markets.

Our Vision

To set the benchmark for quality, efficiency, and reliability in airfreight and cargo logistics across Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

To lead the transformation of logistics through continuous accreditation, full digital integration, and seamless delivery of end-to-end cargo solutions—driven by a commitment to excellence and innovation.

Our Core Services

Cargo Consolidation

Efficient consolidation services that optimize shipping costs while maintaining cargo integrity.

Dangerous Goods Handling

Certified handling and documentation of hazardous materials in compliance with IATA and international safety standards.

Customs Agency & Documentation

End-to-end customs clearance, documentation processing, and regulatory compliance for smooth cargo movement.

GSA (General Sales Agent)

Strategic representation for airlines, offering sales, marketing, and operational support to drive cargo performance.

Other Logistics Solutions

Tailored freight and logistics services designed to meet client-specific requirements.

Humanitarian Aid Logistics

Timely and efficient coordination of relief cargo to support humanitarian missions and disaster response efforts.

Pharmaceutical & Cold Chain Logistics

Secure, temperature-controlled handling of sensitive medical supplies with accredited cold storage partners.

Fresh Fruits & Vegetables Handling

Expert management of perishable cargo, ensuring freshness and compliance from origin to destination.

Special Capability

Temperature-Sensitive Cargo
Everguide Logistics works closely with trusted cold room storage partners within the JKIA ecosystem to provide seamless handling of perishable goods — from fresh produce and flowers to pharmaceuticals — ensuring optimal quality and compliance.

Our Commitment

We are driven by a commitment to efficiency, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Our experienced team ensures every shipment is handled with professionalism and care — from cargo reception to clearance and delivery.
